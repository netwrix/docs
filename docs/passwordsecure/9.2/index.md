# Why Netwrix Password Secure?

Passwords are a constant part of daily business worldwide, and they require professional management. A secure password should be at least 12 characters long and include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and special characters. Ideally, each account uses a unique password that's changed regularly. Meeting these requirements is challenging enough in personal settings — in a large corporate environment, managing passwords adequately without a dedicated tool becomes even harder.

## Scalability

Netwrix Password Secure (NPS) scales to fit small and medium-sized businesses, large enterprises, and global corporations. This flexibility drives its development, allowing it to meet the ever-changing requirements of modern, security-conscious organizations. NPS is a software solution that lets companies effectively manage security-relevant data — including passwords, documents, and certificates — at a very high level of encryption.
