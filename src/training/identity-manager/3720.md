## 3720 Introduction to <NIM />

Recommended prerequisite: 2722 <NIM /> – IGA Project Planning   

The Introduction to <NIM /> course provides a practical overview of how to build, configure, and manage identity data within the platform. It guides learners through loading and structuring identities, navigating the interface, and configuring key components such as profiles, scopes, workflows, and notifications. This course is comprised of 5 modules: 

* Identity Repository 
* Data Model 
* Navigating the Console 
* Profiles & Scopes 
* Workflows & Notifications

Estimated length: 1 hour 45 minutes
