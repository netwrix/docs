## 3860 Introduction to <NPP />

Recommended prerequisite: 2860 <NPP /> – Concepts

The Introduction to <NPP /> course provides an understanding of how to install and setup the application. It covers the licensing tool and three delivery methods for deploying settings in your environment. This application was formerly known as Netwrix Endpoint Policy Manager.
