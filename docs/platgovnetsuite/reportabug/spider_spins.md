# Spider Spins and Doesn't Finish

If you start a spider and the gears on the page spin for more than an hour, the issue is likely with the **IP Restriction**. Contact Netwrix support at
[https://www.netwrix.com/support.html ](https://www.netwrix.com/support.html) for assistance in
configuring your account.
