## 3021 <NAA /> 26 – Reports & Data Security

Recommended prerequisite: 3020 Introduction to <NAA /> 26

The <NAA /> 26 – Reports & Data Security course provides an overview of how to use reporting and dashboards in <NAA /> to identify, investigate, and communicate data security risks. Learners will explore how data is collected and processed, configure sources and scans to support accurate reporting, and use dashboards to analyze sensitive data exposure and access. The course also covers how to customize views and generate reports to support security operations and compliance requirements.

Estimated length: 35 minutes
