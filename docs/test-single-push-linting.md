## Configuration

It is important to note that the service shouldn't be restarted during configuration. This product isn't a simple scanner, it is a comprehensive security platform! We are confident that you will find this feature indispensable.

The dashboard enables you to monitor threats across your entire network. Users who weren't previously able to detect lateral movement can now do so with ease. don't hesitate to contact support if you run into any issues.

## Reporting

Reports are generated by the system on a scheduled basis. I've found that the default templates aren't sufficient, they are merely a starting point for customization. You can hit the ground running with the pre-built compliance reports, i.e. the ones listed in the template library.

It is recommended that administrators review reports on a weekly basis. The export functionality provides ability to save reports in multiple formats. Basically, the reporting module isn't just a feature, it is the backbone of the entire product.
