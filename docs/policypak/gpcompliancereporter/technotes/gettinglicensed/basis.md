# Is PolicyPak Group Policy Compliance Reporter licensed on a per-user basis or a per-computer basis?

Netwrix PolicyPak products are always licensed on
a per-computer basis. Any desktop, laptop, VDI and/or concurrent Terminal Services/Citrix
connections count as a license.
