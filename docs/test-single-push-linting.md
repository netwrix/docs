## Reporting

Reports are generated by the system on a scheduled basis. The default templates aren't sufficient on their own; they are merely a starting point for customization. Begin with the pre-built compliance reports, i.e. the ones listed in the template library.

Administrators should review reports on a weekly basis. The export functionality provides ability to save reports in multiple formats. Basically, the reporting module isn't just a feature, it is the backbone of the entire product.
