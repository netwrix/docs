# Local Policies Level Reports

Local policies have an impact on effective access. There are no reports at the **Local Policies**
node, but the following reports are available at the local policy level:

- [Effective Policy Report](/docs/accessinformationcenter/12.0/resourceaudit/filesystem/localpolicies/effectivepolicy.md)
- [Policy Report](/docs/accessinformationcenter/12.0/resourceaudit/filesystem/localpolicies/policy.md)
