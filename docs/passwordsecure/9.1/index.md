# Why Netwrix Password Secure?

Passwords are central to daily business operations worldwide and require professional management. A secure password should have at least 12 characters and include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and special characters. Ideally, each account uses a unique password that's changed regularly. Meeting these requirements is challenging enough in personal settings — in a large corporate environment, managing passwords effectively without a dedicated tool is nearly impossible.

## Scalability

Netwrix Password Secure (NPS) scales to fit organizations of any size, from SMEs to global corporations. Its flexibility is central to its design, allowing it to adapt to the ever-changing requirements of modern, security-conscious companies. NPS helps organizations effectively manage security-sensitive data — including passwords, documents, and certificates — at a high level of encryption.
