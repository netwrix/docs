## 4000 <NAA /> – Basic Troubleshooting

Recommended prerequisite: 3000 Introduction to <NAA />

The <NAA /> – Basic Troubleshooting course course teaches administrators how to systematically diagnose and resolve common issues in <NAA />. Topics include how <NAA /> interacts with SQL Server, how service accounts and connection profiles affect job behavior, and how to interpret job status tables and error messages. Learners will also explore the <NAA /> logging system and learn how to collect diagnostic information when escalating to <Company /> Support. This product was formerly named Netwrix Enterprise Auditor / StealthAUDIT.

Estimated length: 50 minutes
