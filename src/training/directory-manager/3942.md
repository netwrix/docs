## 3942 <NDM /> – Portal Design

Recommended prerequisite: 3941 <NDM /> – Identity Stores

The <NDM /> – Portal Design course provides information on aspects of an application portal and how to configure and design its layout. This course explains how Application Portals are structured and customized in <NDM /> and explores how to customize portal components to support the organization’s requirements.

Estimated length: 45 minutes
