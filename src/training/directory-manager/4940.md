## 4940 <NDM /> – Basic Troubleshooting

Recommended prerequisite: 3942 <NDM /> – Portal Design

The 4940 <NDM /> – Basic Troubleshooting course provides an introduction to foundational troubleshooting tasks in <NDM />. It explains how to check key services, review replication status, assess Identity Store health, verify IIS availability, and locate the logs used to investigate system issues.

Estimated length: 30 minutes
